Two strangers discover they share psychic dreams of crimes in progress — she experiences the victims, he the perpetrators — and as the cases escalate, the darkness they absorb threatens to destroy them and the bond forming between them.

<!--more-->

The core theme explores the cost of witness — what happens to people who refuse to look away from suffering, and whether love can survive the weight of what they've seen. The dreams bypass language entirely, arriving as sensation, temperature, smell, and the weight of hands.
